QooBot Max

Industrial flagship for manufacturing & research

Full-size bipedal humanoid robot with full-body force control, dexterous hand manipulation, and autonomous navigation. Powered by qoobrain OS with 100 TOPS compute — redefining what industrial robots can do. Available in Deep Gray, Titanium Blue, and Midnight Black.
