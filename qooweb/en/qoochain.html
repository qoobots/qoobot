🏭 qoochain

Robot Manufacturing Supply Chain System — providing standardized production processes, quality inspection, factory calibration, and BOM management to ensure manufacturability and consistency of robot products.

Architecture Design Complete Alpha In Development
Project Overview

Standardized Robot Manufacturing

qoochain defines the complete supply chain management system for QooBot robots, from component procurement to final product delivery, ensuring quality and consistency at every stage. It provides transparent manufacturing standards and quality control processes for manufacturers, integrators, and developers.

Module 01
📋

BOM Reference Design

Provides complete bill of materials, supplier information, alternatives, and cost analysis to support mass production.

Module 02
🏭

Manufacturing Standards

Defines standardized specifications for assembly processes, process requirements, equipment configuration, and production line layout.

Module 03
⚙️

Factory Calibration System

A complete solution for automated calibration processes, parameter configuration, functional testing, and performance verification.

Module 04
🔍

Quality Inspection System

A multi-level quality management system covering defect detection, data analysis, and continuous improvement.

Module 05
📦

Supply Chain Management

A supply chain solution covering supplier management, inventory control, logistics optimization, and risk early warning.

Module 06
📊

Traceability & Compliance

Full product lifecycle traceability, compliance verification, document management, and audit support.
