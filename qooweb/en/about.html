About Us

Lowering the barrier to robot innovation, so everyone can help build the future of physical AI.

Just as Linux did for computing — QooBot is doing the same for robotics.

Our Mission

The robotics industry is trapped in fragmentation — every robot runs proprietary software, algorithms can't be reused across platforms, and developers are forced to reinvent the wheel. This closed ecosystem severely hinders the progress of physical AI.

QooBot was born to break this cycle. We're building a universal operating system for all robots — One Brain, Any Body, Everywhere.

Our Philosophy

"Qoo" stands for Cool — we believe robots should be exciting, cutting-edge, and full of creativity. "Bot" represents who we serve — robots of all forms. We're committed to making robot development cool and simple.
