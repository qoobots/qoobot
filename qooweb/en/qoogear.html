Ecosystem Layer · Gear Ecosystem

🔌 qoogear

Robot Accessory Ecosystem — providing third-party peripheral certification, standardized interfaces, communication protocols, and quality assurance to build an open and trusted robot accessory ecosystem.

Architecture Design Complete Alpha In Development
Project Overview

Unified Standards for the Robot Accessory Ecosystem

qoogear defines the unified interfaces, certification standards, and communication protocols for QooBot robot accessories. It ensures third-party accessories meet the highest standards of performance, safety, and compatibility, providing robots with unlimited expansion possibilities.

Module 01
🔍

Accessory Certification System

Establish certification processes, testing standards, and quality assurance for third-party accessories, ensuring compatibility and safety.

Module 02
🔌

Standardized Interfaces

Define unified standards for mechanical, electrical, and communication interfaces, simplifying accessory development and integration.

Module 03
🤖

End Effectors

Support multiple end effectors including dexterous hands, grippers, and tool heads, covering diverse application scenarios.

Module 04
⌚

Wearable Devices

Integrate head-mounted displays, haptic feedback gloves, motion capture suits, and other wearable accessories to enhance human-robot interaction.

Module 05
📡

Communication Protocol Stack

Provide a unified protocol stack for accessory communication, supporting wired, wireless, near-field, and other connectivity options.

Module 06
🛡️

Safety & Compliance

Ensure all accessories comply with EMC, electrical safety, data privacy, and other regulatory requirements.

Key Features

Building an Open Accessory Ecosystem

🔌

Plug & Play

Support automatic recognition, driver loading, and configuration of accessories — no manual installation or setup required.

🛡️

Safety Certified

All accessories must pass rigorous safety and compatibility certification to ensure system stability.

🌐

Open Standards

Publicly available interface specifications and protocol documentation, supporting third-party manufacturers to freely develop accessories.

📈

Ecosystem Expansion

Support broad expansion from industrial tools to consumer accessories, meeting diverse application scenario needs.

🔄

Backward Compatible

Ensure compatibility of new accessories with older system versions, protecting user investment.

🔍

Quality Assured

Rigorous testing processes and quality standards to ensure the reliability and durability of every accessory.
