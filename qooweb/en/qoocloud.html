Project Overview

The Intelligent Hub for Robot Cloud

qoocloud is the cloud service platform of the QooBot ecosystem, extending robot computing, storage, and management capabilities to the cloud for unlimited robot capability expansion.

Service 01
🚀

Remote Inference Service

Offloads compute-intensive AI tasks to the cloud, providing powerful model inference capabilities.

Service 02
🏢

Cluster Management

Unified management of multiple robot nodes, enabling resource scheduling, task allocation, and status monitoring.

Service 03
🔄

OTA Upgrades

Seamless remote upgrades of firmware, software, and models, with canary releases and rollback mechanisms.

Service 04
💾

Data Synchronization

Real-time synchronization, backup, analysis, and visualization of robot data.

Service 05
🎭

Multi-Robot Orchestration

Coordinate multiple robots to complete complex tasks, enabling swarm intelligence and collaborative operations.

Service 06
🔒

Security & Compliance

End-to-end encryption, access control, audit logging, compliance certification, and other security services.
