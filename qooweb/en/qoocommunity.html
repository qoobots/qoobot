Ecosystem Layer · Global Community

👥 qoocommunity

A global collaboration network for robot developers, researchers, and enthusiasts — promoting the popularization and advancement of robotics through open-source contributions, knowledge sharing, and event organization.

Active Continuously Expanding
Project Overview

Connecting Global Robotics Enthusiasts

qoocommunity is the community hub of the QooBot ecosystem, dedicated to building an open, inclusive, and collaborative global robotics community. Through online platforms, offline events, educational programs, and more, it fosters knowledge dissemination and technical exchange.

Module 01
🌍

Global Contributor Network

Connecting developers, researchers, students, and enterprises worldwide to establish cross-regional collaboration.

Module 02
🎤

Annual Developer Conference

Hosting global robotics technology conferences to share the latest research, practical experience, and future trends.

Module 03
🏫

University Partnership Program

Establishing partnerships with global universities, providing course materials, experimental platforms, and research support.

Module 04
👑

Ambassador Program

Selecting community leaders as ambassadors to promote robotics, organize local events, and provide technical support.

Module 05
📚

Education Resource Library

Providing tutorials, case studies, video courses, and other educational resources to lower the barrier to robotics learning.

Module 06
🏆

Competitions & Challenges

Organizing robotics competitions, hackathons, and innovation challenges to spark creativity and practical skills.
