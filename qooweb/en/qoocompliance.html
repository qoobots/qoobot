Project Overview

The Compliance Guide for Robotics Industry

qoocompliance is the regulatory compliance project of the QooBot ecosystem, providing regulatory guidance and compliance support for every phase of robot design, manufacturing, deployment, and operations.

Domain 01
🛡️

Safety Standards

Interpretation and implementation guides for ISO 10218, ISO 13482, UL, and other robot safety standards.

Domain 02
📜

Compliance Certification

Process and requirement guides for CE, FCC, RoHS, REACH, and other product certifications.

Domain 03
🌐

Export Control

Compliance guidance for EAR, ITAR, China Export Controls, and other cross-border trade regulations.

Domain 04
🔒

Privacy Regulations

Compliance frameworks for GDPR, CCPA, PIPL, LGPD, and other global privacy regulations.

Domain 05
⚖️

Ethics Guidelines

Robot ethics principles, AI governance frameworks, and ethical guidance for responsible innovation.

Domain 06
📊

Compliance Toolkit

Practical tools including compliance checklists, risk assessment templates, document generators, and audit guides.
