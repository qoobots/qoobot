🔧 qoosvc

Robot Foundational Service Suite — providing key system services including voice assistant, spatial understanding, navigation, multi-robot connectivity, and self-diagnostics.

Design Phase Prototype In Development
Project Overview

Robot Foundational Capabilities as Services

qoosvc is the system services platform of the QooBot ecosystem, encapsulating commonly used robot foundational capabilities as standardized services for quick integration and invocation by upper-layer applications.

Service 01
🗣️

Voice Assistant Service

Complete voice interaction capabilities including speech recognition, natural language understanding, speech synthesis, and dialogue management.

Service 02
🗺️

Spatial Understanding Service

Intelligent environment perception capabilities including environment modeling, object recognition, scene understanding, and spatial reasoning.

Service 03
🧭

Navigation Service

Autonomous navigation capabilities including path planning, obstacle avoidance, localization tracking, and map building.

Service 04
🔗

Multi-Robot Connectivity

Multi-robot collaboration capabilities including inter-robot communication, task coordination, data sharing, and swarm coordination.

Service 05
🔍

Self-Diagnostics Service

System self-healing capabilities including health monitoring, fault detection, performance analysis, and preventive maintenance.

Service 06
⚙️

System Management Service

System operations capabilities including configuration management, log collection, performance monitoring, and resource scheduling.
